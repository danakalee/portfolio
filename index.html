about me

Hi, me

I'm all about putting in more than I'm taking out.

Looking around and seeing all the problems in the world I decided to start looking for the people solving them.
I found that they're talking at meet ups in languages with symbols and hashes.
I scratched my head at loops and chomps and decided I better dive in.

Currently enrolled with Coder Academy at the Brisbane campus.
Graduting August 2019 as a full stack developer.

Formerly a professional cat herder at Australia Post leading a team of 15.
I made the move to big business after a decade in the beer soaked world of hospitality wrangling cowboys and chefs in North Queensland.

I have my sights set on being a quality work horse with technical sophostication.
Aiming for the title of Performance Penalty Shoot Out Queen.

Whole heartedly looking forward to being involved with something that is greater than the sum of it's parts.
